Why Study in Malaysia?

Why Study in Malaysia

A Malaysian degree gives you more than an academic experience which helps you in your real-world experiences. Wherever you go in the world, a Malaysian education opens the door of your career and practical life ahead.

  • Ranked universities: In Malaysia, you can find a number of internationally ranked universities. It has a high number of ranked institutes such as University of Malaya, Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia, and Universiti Sains Malaysia. Many top universities are based in Malaysia.
  • Education quality: The standard of education in Malaysia has always been a center point attraction for international students. Malaysian universities take a more practical approach rather than lecture-based learning for international students. Students are expected to take a proactive role and apply the skills in real-world scenarios. This allows deep understanding of your course which eventually benefits you in the long term.
  • Multicultural and secure: For international students, the diversity is reflected in every aspect of Malaysian culture, which has ranked as a top multicultural and secure country for international students. The diversity is reflected in every aspect of Malaysian culture, food, literature, arts, and music.
  • Language options: English is widely used in Malaysian universities, making it accessible for international students. Many programs are offered entirely in English.

Average Tuition Fees in Malaysia for International Students

Tuition fees in Malaysia for international students vary depending on the university and program. The average tuition fees for undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Malaysia range from MYR 15,000 - MYR 30,000 per year.

Working Hours Allowed in Malaysia (Student Visa)

As per Malaysian regulations, international students on a student visa are allowed to work part-time up to 20 hours per week during semester breaks or holidays. Working during the academic session is generally not permitted unless special permission is granted.

Average wage rates in Malaysia for international students are MYR 5-10/hour.

Living Cost in Malaysia for International Students

The average living cost for an international student in Malaysia depends on the city you are living in; the living cost in Kuala Lumpur is higher compared to other cities.

  • MYR 1,500 per month for living in major cities like Kuala Lumpur
  • MYR 1,000 per month for living in smaller cities

If you have a student loan or a government sponsorship, you will need to submit the evidence while submitting your visa application.
