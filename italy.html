Why Study in Italy?

Why Study in Italy

An Italian degree gives you more than an academic experience which helps you in your real-world experiences. Wherever you go in the world, an Italian education opens the door of your career and practical life ahead.

  • Ranked universities: In Italy, you can find a number of internationally ranked universities. It has a high number of ranked institutes such as University of Bologna, Sapienza University of Rome, and University of Padua. Many top universities are based in Italy.
  • Education quality: The standard of education in Italy has always been a center point attraction for international students. Italian universities take a more practical approach rather than lecture-based learning for international students. Students are expected to take a proactive role and apply the skills in real-world scenarios. This allows deep understanding of your course which eventually benefits you in the long term.
  • Multicultural and secure: For international students, the diversity is reflected in every aspect of Italian culture, which has ranked as a top multicultural and secure country for international students. The diversity is reflected in every aspect of Italian culture, food, literature, arts, and music.
  • Language options: While Italian is the primary language, many programs are offered in English, especially at the postgraduate level, making it accessible for international students. Learning Italian can also enhance your experience.
