Why Study in UAE?

Why Study in UAE

A UAE degree gives you more than an academic experience which helps you in your real-world experiences. Wherever you go in the world, a UAE education opens the door of your career and practical life ahead.

  • Ranked universities: In the UAE, you can find a number of internationally ranked universities. It has a high number of ranked institutes such as Khalifa University, United Arab Emirates University, and University of Sharjah. Many top universities are based in the UAE.
  • Education quality: The standard of education in the UAE has always been a center point attraction for international students. UAE universities take a more practical approach rather than lecture-based learning for international students. Students are expected to take a proactive role and apply the skills in real-world scenarios. This allows deep understanding of your course which eventually benefits you in the long term.
  • Multicultural and secure: For international students, the diversity is reflected in every aspect of UAE culture, which has ranked as a top multicultural and secure country for international students. The diversity is reflected in every aspect of UAE culture, food, literature, arts, and music.
  • Language options: English is widely used in UAE universities, making it accessible for international students. Many programs are offered entirely in English.

Average Tuition Fees in UAE for International Students

Tuition fees in the UAE for international students vary depending on the university and program. The average tuition fees for undergraduate and postgraduate programs in the UAE range from AED 40,000 - AED 80,000 per year.

Working Hours Allowed in UAE (Student Visa)

As per UAE regulations, international students on a student visa are allowed to work part-time up to 15 hours per week during academic sessions and full-time during breaks, subject to university approval. Working more than the allowed hours can affect your visa status.

Average wage rates in the UAE for international students are AED 20-30/hour.

Living Cost in UAE for International Students

The average living cost for an international student in the UAE depends on the city you are living in; the living cost in Dubai or Abu Dhabi is higher compared to other emirates.

  • AED 3,000 per month for living in major cities like Dubai or Abu Dhabi
  • AED 2,000 per month for living in smaller emirates

If you have a student loan or a government sponsorship, you will need to submit the evidence while submitting your visa application.
